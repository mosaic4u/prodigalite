Meet the Principal: Nancy M. Siew, O.Ont.

Former Citizenship Judge • Hon. Lt.-Colonel • Order of Ontario Recipient

Nancy Siew offers a unique "best of both worlds" perspective that bridges cultural divides. Raised in Hong Kong and educated across Switzerland and Canada, her ability to build bridges through etiquette and social grace has been her greatest asset in both business and social spheres.

A distinguished public servant, Nancy’s contributions have been recognized at the highest levels. She is a recipient of the Order of Ontario, the Queen’s Golden Jubilee Medal, the Queen’s Diamond Jubilee Medal, and most recently, the King Charles III Coronation Medal. These honors reflect her lifelong commitment to civic duty and the refinement of the Canadian social fabric.

As a former Citizenship Judge and Honorary Lieutenant-Colonel, Nancy has mentored high-stakes professionals, including politicians, executives, bankers, and MBA students. Her didactic sessions are more than just etiquette lessons—they are masterclasses in "Social Intelligence," designed to give mentees the tools to exude social grace at all occasions, opening doors to prestigious careers and social circles.

Nancy believes that social grace is a universal language. Her Swiss-based curriculum combines the precision of Western protocol with the depth of Oriental virtues, ensuring that her students can "walk with kings—nor lose the common touch."
